About App

StudyNShort is an Android application developed for the Bachelor of Computer Application Degree (BCA) VI semester. It helps students organize and retrieve study materials by electronically downloading, storing, and managing study notes. The app offers various features, such as organizing notes by subject, topic, or course, and is compatible with mobile devices for easy access. The app also provides an authentication system to ensure only authorized users can access the notes, ensuring their safety and security.
